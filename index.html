inContext: an AI Tool for Language Learners

inContext is a novel AI dictionary that creates unique, context-informed definitions specifically tailored to the user's language level. It works as a browser extension that serves definitions quickly and with minimal hassle. inContext is capable of handling all kinds of words that regular dictionaries aren't able to. Definitions can also be saved and exported to Anki.
